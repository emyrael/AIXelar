# AIXELAR Overview

AIXELAR is an AI product studio and software engineering company that designs and builds intelligent digital products for businesses.

AIXELAR builds AI-powered applications, custom web apps, mobile apps, SaaS platforms, AI agents, workflow automation systems, dashboards, data platforms, cloud solutions, API integrations, enterprise software, and product strategy.

The core philosophy is simple: AIXELAR does not begin with technology. AIXELAR begins by understanding the client's business.

Before recommending tools, AIXELAR learns how the company operates, how teams work, where bottlenecks exist, what data matters, and which workflows are slowing the business down. Then AIXELAR designs and builds software that fits the business instead of forcing the business to change around generic software.

## Secure Client Portals

AIXELAR can build secure client portals that give teams and clients a private, controlled interface for accessing automation, data, reports, and dashboards. AIXELAR can also implement WhatsApp automation, webhooks, email notifications, or other channels depending on the use case.

Portal capabilities can include:

- Private URL access such as `/monitoring` or `portal.clientdomain.com`
- Uploading and approving data pulls
- Triggering workflows on demand
- Viewing real-time KPIs and dashboards
- Downloading reports as PDF, Excel, or CSV
- Setting custom alerts and notifications
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- Optional SSO and MFA for enhanced security

Security posture:

- Authentication with login/password or SSO
- Role-based access control for admins, managers, and viewers
- Optional multi-factor authentication
- Encrypted data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Encrypted data at rest where required
- Audit logs tracking who accessed what and when
- Client-controlled data retention policies

AIXELAR is not only a chatbot or automation vendor. It builds connected systems that bring data, workflows, AI, dashboards, and access control into one controlled business environment.

## How AIXELAR Differs From Generic AI Tools

Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other AI assistants are useful for one-off questions and quick tasks. AIXELAR builds always-connected systems for ongoing business operations.

AIXELAR systems can connect to live data sources, run scheduled reports, monitor workflows, enforce access control, and produce repeatable outputs. Generic AI tools usually require a user to upload files manually and ask a new prompt each time.

Key differences:

- One-off file upload vs always-connected business system
- Individual prompt answers vs repeatable workflows
- Manual reporting vs scheduled automated reporting
- Single-user usage vs team dashboards and approvals
- Limited governance vs RBAC, audit logs, SSO, and MFA options
- Unclear operational ownership vs client-controlled data retention and storage options

The chatbot should explain this difference clearly and without attacking generic AI tools. The point is that generic AI tools are helpful, but AIXELAR builds governed, integrated, business-ready systems.
